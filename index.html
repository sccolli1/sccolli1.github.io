About XR Art

XR Art is a Parent Company which oversees multiple Augmented and Virtual Reality Projects across the globe. Our mission is to enhance entertainment expereinces by making art, animation, and new worlds come to life.

Whether you're are an adventurous exploring out projects, a designer looking to become a part of our current projects, or a graphic artist looking to share your projects, XR Art is the perfect place for you!
